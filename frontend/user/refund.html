一、可退款情况

  • 商品严重损坏、腐烂、变质，影响正常食用。
  • 收到的商品与订单明显不符（数量错误、品种错误、严重缺斤少两）。
  • 配送错误：送错地址、整单漏送等情况。

二、不可退款情况

  • 因个人口味、主观感受导致的不满意（例如：不喜欢口感）。
  • 商品已被大量食用，无法判断质量问题。
  • 收货后超过 24 小时才反馈的，一般不再受理退款（特殊情况除外）。

三、申请时限与凭证

  • 请在收货后 24 小时内通过微信客服或订单页面联系客服。
  • 请尽量提供清晰的照片（商品整体、问题细节、包装标签等），帮助我们快速核实。

四、退款方式

  • 测试阶段：退款将通过微信转账或账户余额形式返还。
  • 正式上线后：如使用在线支付，我们会尽量原路退回（以支付渠道规则为准）。

五、部分退款与补发

  • 如订单中只有部分商品存在问题，我们通常采取部分退款或补发送货的方式。
  • 对于明显因操作失误造成的错误（例如整单漏送），我们将与您协商补发或全额退款。

六、解释权

English Version

1. Eligible Refund Cases

  • Serious damage, spoilage, or quality issues that make the food not suitable for consumption.
  • Significant mismatch with the order (wrong items, wrong quantity, missing items).
  • Delivery mistakes such as wrong address or missing the whole order.

2. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Dissatisfaction due to personal taste or preference only (e.g. "don’t like the flavor").
  • The product has been mostly consumed and quality issues cannot be reasonably verified.
  • Complaints made more than 24 hours after delivery may not be accepted, except in special circumstances.

3. Timeframe and Evidence

  • Please contact us within 24 hours after delivery through WeChat or customer support.
  • Please provide clear photos (overall product, defects, packaging labels) to help us verify the issue quickly.

4. Refund Methods

  • During the testing phase, refunds may be issued via WeChat transfer or store credit.
  • After we enable online payments, we will refund to the original payment method where possible, subject to the payment provider’s rules.

5. Partial Refunds and Re-Delivery

  • If only part of the order has issues, we may provide a partial refund or re-delivery for those items.
  • For obvious operational mistakes (e.g. missing the entire order), we will work with you on full refund or re-delivery.

6. Interpretation

Freshbuy reserves the right of final interpretation of this refund policy within reasonable and fair principles. We will always try to resolve disputes through friendly communication.
